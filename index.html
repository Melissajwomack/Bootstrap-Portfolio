About Me

Served eight years in the U.S. Army, gaining skills necessary to work in a team, discipline, and the ability to pay attention to detail. Later, earned a bachelor's degree in microbiology with honors and became a science enthusiast. Both paths were rewarding, but found my true calling in technology. Always thinking analytically and methodically solving problems, programming was a natural fit. Now, with experience in web development technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I have a passion to create novel and valuable web applications.
